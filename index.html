Our mission is to improve the lives of disabled and disadvantaged people through sailing programs which develop interpersonal and team skills to assist them to meet life challenges with determination and optimism.

Our Programs

We are proud to have been offering our sailing programs for over 25 years

We provide free sailing and training programs for people with a disability, disadvantaged children and adults, and their carers. Our programs use sailing as a tool to build confidence, self-esteem and new relationships. Through sailing, people start to forget about what they can't do and focus on what they can do.
