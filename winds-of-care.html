Winds of Care

A Chance to Take a Break

The Winds of Care program offers carers and families of people with a disability or disadvantage the chance to take a bit of time out for themselves while on a relaxing harbour sail.

Key Outcomes

The program gives carers of people with a disability or disadvantage the chance to take a break from their daily lives.

Our participants:

  • Take time out from their caring roles
  • Network with other carers on the sail
  • Steer the boat and learn some basic sailing skills

About Our Yachts

Our yachts are specially adapted to take wheelchairs. Using a electronic joystick, pretty much everyone on board can have a go at steering the boat. Our skippers and volunteers are trained to offer support, kindness, and a positive environment which encourages our participants and stimulates their imaginations.
