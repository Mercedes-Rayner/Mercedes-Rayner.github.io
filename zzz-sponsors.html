Our Sponsors and Supporters

Sponsors

Australian Maritime Technologies (AMT) is an independent, Australian staff owned, design and marine engineering consultancy. AMT provide a full breadth of multidisciplinary naval ship design, systems integration and systems engineering services to Defence and Defence industry customers. Through their Social Responsibility Program, AMT provide funding and engaging members of their staff in our Winds of Joy programs in Melbourne.

Survitec is a global leader in survival and safety solutions to the marine, defence, aviation and offshore industries. Survitec generously provides us with an extensive range of RFD safety equipment. Servitec recognised that both adults and children with disabilities need a heightened level of safety awareness, and the appropriate RFD equipment to ensure their personal safety. Our safety equipment is kept up-to-date and in good working order by the service team at Survitec Australia in Wetherill Park.

Woolwich Dock is a modern, one-stop, environmentally-friendly maritime centre, making it one of the most attractive destinations on the harbour for boat owners who want professional advice, a comprehensive range of services, and for those who prefer to do the work themselves. A huge thank you to the Oatley family for supporting the Wright of Passage Youth Program by providing us with a place to house our wooden boat, Wright of Passage Mercator, while the youth undertake the repairs and restoration work.

Windcraft sells the best yachts in the world, penned by the best yacht designers in the world. Windcraft is your one-stop shop for yacht sales. Their large range gives you exceptional choice. Choose from Hanse, Moody, Dehler, Solaris and Shipman. Experienced Sales Teams in Australia and New Zealand. Their experienced yacht sales team are passionate about yachts and yachting… and passionate about making sure you choose the right yacht for you. If you choose the right yacht you’ll enjoy wonderful times on the water.

JLL is a world leader in real estate services, powered by an entrepreneurial spirit. We want the most ambitious clients to work with us, and the most ambitious people to work for us. It’s as simple as that. JLL buy, build, occupy and invest in a variety of assets including industrial, commercial, retail, residential and hotel real estate. From tech startups to global firms, our clients span industries including banking, energy, healthcare, law, life sciences, manufacturing and technology.
JLL is a financial supporter of our programs.

National Pump & Energy (NPE) are one of Australia’s leading pump, power and compressed air hire equipment specialists. NPE prides itself on its knowledge, experience and reliable solutions for our customers. NPE have the unique ability to be responsive, flexible and provide technical expertise to meet the specialised needs of every job. NPE specialise in wellpoint dewatering, mine pit dewatering, borehole pumping, remote and back up power generation to name a few. NPE is a financial supporter of our programs.

International Yacht Paint helps you paint your boat like a pro - find the best products to keep your boat in great condition, get all the support you need to paint with confidence, and benefit from their continuous innovation and scientific expertise. A division of AkzoNobel, International is one of the world's largest manufacturers of high performance boat paint, yacht coatings and marine protection. Our race yacht WOT EVA is anti-fouled by International Yacht Paint using their product VC Offshore.

Supporters

AFLOAT Magazine was first published in November 1989 as Sydney AFLOAT. It has grown to be Australia's favourite monthly boating magazine, with the print magazine alone being distributed to over 500 locations around the country. AFLOAT support us by publishing our articles and providing us with advertising space.

The ASX Refinitiv Charity Foundation raises funds for Australian-based children's, disability, and medical research charities via some of corporate Australia's most anticipated fundraising events. We are one of the charities that benefits from the fundraising work of the Charity Foundation.

Bob from Bob Scott Marine Trimming (0418 160 249) donates his time and skills to assist with maintaining all the marine trimmings on our four Sydney-based yachts including fenders, boom covers, rope bags etc, as well as a complete replacement of Wright of Passage Mecator's bimini cover.

Proudly Australian and privately owned, since 1998, Built has grown to be a national diversified general contractor and one of Australia’s largest private construction groups. They've built their reputation on being the most responsive, client focused partner in the industry with extensive Tier 1 and large corporation experience.

The City of Melbourne is the local government authority for central Melbourne and the surrounding suburbs. They support us in Melbourne by generously providing a free berth at Docklands.

The Cruising Yacht Club of Australia (CYCA) is Australia's premier yacht club and home to what is considered a yachting 'Everest' - the Rolex Sydney Hobart Yacht Race

The Derwent Sailing Squadron (DSS) is a yacht club located on the spectacular banks of the Derwent River, in Sandy Bay, Hobart (TAS). The DSS welcomes our Winds of Joy program to the Hobart each year, providing our yacht free berthing. We also thank the DSS members and patrons for accommodating us and our clients with warm hospitality as we run our programs out of the marina.

Marine SafetyWorks provide assistance to all yacht owners to improve safety awareness, and to ensure compliance with relevant safety regulations. Principle instructor Genevieve supports us by offering a group discount to her courses for our volunteers. She is highly experienced and has supported many of our sailors with a disability or life challenge with respect and dignity through her courses

MySail is an Australian-based, web platform that connects crew and yachts to help you to get out on the water more often. MySail has teamed up with us to help  sell berths on our yacht Kayle for the 75th Rolex Sydney Hobart Yacht Race

P&L Corporate Communications is a specialised financial communications firm. P&L's attention to every aspect of a transaction, along with their unrivalled access to reporters, columnists and editors, has allowed them to work with many of the best known companies in Australia and overseas. Ian Pemberton provides his services in connecting us with the media and offering media relations advice.

Quest Apartment Hotels has over 150 properties in Australia, New Zealand and Fiji. They are located in central business districts, suburban and regional areas with close proximity to head offices, business centres and key tourist destinations.

Located on Felix Bay at Point Piper in Sydney’s Eastern Suburbs, the Royal Prince Edward Yacht Club was formed in 1922 by a group of keen Sydney Harbour sailors. It is a private yacht club with membership by invitation only. Members are primarily keelboat sailors and a group of Masters level dinghy enthusiasts.

SailTime offers an innovative new approach to sailing and boat ownership that makes it easier, quicker and more economical for aspiring or experienced skippers to take the helm of a beautiful, well-equipped sail or powerboat.

Women Who Sail Australia (WWSA) is a women's-only Facebook Group for women who are involved with boats and who love the ocean. The WWSA donate the proceeds from their annual Gathering on The Bay conference to our programs, as well as provide a platform for our women volunteers to raise awareness for our organization.
