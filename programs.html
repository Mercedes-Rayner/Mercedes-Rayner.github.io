Our Programs

Winds of Joy

Through 'Winds of Joy' we start to create change. It's the first step for many on the journey towards accepting their circumstances and believing they can achieve more.

Find out more
Winds of Care

The Winds of Care program offers the the fabulous people who commit their lives to caring for others who are disabled or disadvantaged the chance to take time out for themselves.

Find out more
Winds of Change

Youth Program
Winds of Change opens a whole new world of challenge, skill development and discovery for young people who have disengaged from learning.

Find out more
Wright of Passage

Youth Program
Wright of Passage is a workplace-learning program designed to support and grow the personal and social development skills of young people.

Find out more
Yacht Racding

The Racing Program provides opportunities for people to challenge themselves and continue building their sailing experience further on from one of our other four sailing programs.

Find out more
