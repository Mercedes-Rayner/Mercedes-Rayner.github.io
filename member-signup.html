Become a Member

Thank you for considering becoming a member of Sailors with Disabilities. By becoming a member you help us to provide free sailing and training programs for people with a disability, disadvantaged children and adults, and their carers. You will receive regular email newsletter updates on our activities and invitations to supporter events. Membership will be automatically renewed on 1 January each year.
