Our annual fundraising event, the Making Waves Regatta is returning in March 2020

When: Friday, 6 March 2020 - 12 pm until sunset
Where: Cruising Yacht Club of Australia, Rushcutters Bay
Download the Regatta flyer

This is your opportunity to sail on the magnificent Sydney Harbour and enjoy an after-race party at the Cruising Yacht Club of Australia, all while raising much needed funds for the charity Sailors with disABILITIES.

The afternoon comprises of:

  • a packed lunch and drinks for you to have on board before the race
  • a yacht race around Sydney Harbour, run by the Race Officers of the Cruising Yacht Club of Australia
  • food, drinks and entertainment at the after-party event in the yacht club
  • trophies for the winner of each race division
  • the opportunity to further support the fundraising effort by purchasing tickets in the raffle or Wine Wall
View the photos from the 2019 Rally

How to Enter

Entry options cater for corporate groups, syndicates or individuals. All food, drinks, race entry and after-party is included.

To enter, download one of the entry forms below and return the completed form to events@sailorswithdisabilities.com or post to Sailors with disABILITIES, Level 1, 1C New Beach Road, Darling Point, NSW, 2027. For enquires, please contact us on 0421 725 170 or events@sailorswithdisabilities.com.

We need your help

We need your help to continue our mission of building optimism and opportunities for Australians living with a disability or challenging circumstances. Funds raised from the regatta goes towards providing our five core programs to participants free of charge.
